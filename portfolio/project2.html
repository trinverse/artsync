RDACTD

Complaint Management & Business Accountability Platform

RDACTD is a full-stack cross-platform application for anonymous complaint submission, business accountability scoring, and dispute resolution.

Overview

RDACTD is a SaaS platform that empowers consumers to file complaints against businesses while giving companies tools to respond, resolve disputes, and improve their accountability scores. The platform supports anonymous submissions, real-time messaging with end-to-end encryption, AI-powered insights, and a tiered subscription model for both consumers and businesses.

Key Features

Anonymous Complaint Filing Evidence upload, category taxonomy, and status tracking with full privacy protection.
Business Dashboards Multi-location management, predictive analytics, score improvement recommendations, and enhanced analytics.
Real-Time Messaging Encrypted chat between complainants and businesses with quick actions and analytics.
AI-Powered Insights AI chat assistant, smart complaint form suggestions, and predictive analytics for businesses.
Company Search & Profiles Public business profiles with accountability scores, reviews, and complaint history.
Subscription System 5-tier model (Free through Enterprise at $149/mo) with feature gating, Stripe billing, and team management.
Enterprise Features SSO configuration, white-label support, API access, and dedicated support.
Admin Panel User management, business claim review, audit logs, analytics dashboards, and unverified company moderation.
